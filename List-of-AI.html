Free, Useful and list of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

By Bhaskar Sah | November 11, 2025 | Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI tools are quickly emerging in many occupations, whether you're a student, creator, developer, entrepreneur, or business owner. The most convenient part is that a majority of these tools are free! They help with writing, image generation, research, coding, productivity, design, translation, and much more. The following guide contains a thorough and well-structured list of free AI tools, their links, and descriptions that can help you conveniently navigate the tools and select those that suit your needs.

1. General AI Assistants

  • ChatGPT (Free): Multi-purpose AI for writing, coding, analysis, and learning.
  • Google Gemini Free: AI assistant integrated with Google Search and services.
  • Microsoft Copilot Free: Windows + Office AI assistant for daily tasks and automation.
  • Perplexity AI Free: Research-focused AI with real-time citations and facts.
  • HuggingChat: Open-source chatbot alternative.

2. Writing & Content Creation

  • QuillBot: Paraphrasing and rewriting tool.
  • Grammarly: Grammar and style improvement.
  • Rytr: AI writer for blogs and scripts.
  • HIX.AI: Multi-purpose writing assistant.
  • Notion AI: Writing and productivity enhancer.

3. AI Image Generators

  • Craiyon (DALL·E Mini): Free image generator.
  • Bing Image Creator: Free DALL·E 3 image creation.
  • Stable Diffusion: Open-source model for image generation.
  • Leonardo AI: High-quality creative images.
  • Canva AI: Image + design editing platform.

4. AI Video Generators

  • CapCut AI: AI video editing and captions.
  • Runway ML: AI video generation & VFX.
  • Pika Labs: Text-to-video tool.
  • InVideo: Script-to-video creation.
  • Luma Dream Machine: Cinematic AI video generator.

5. AI Voice & Audio Tools

  • ElevenLabs: Natural text-to-speech AI.
  • Voicify AI: Voice cloning & conversion.
  • Audacity: Free audio editor with AI support plugins.
  • Suno AI: AI tool for music creation.
  • AIVA: AI composer for music production.

6. Design, Art & Creative Tools

  • Canva: Graphic design suite with AI support.
  • Autodesk Sketchbook: Professional drawing tool.
  • Krita: Digital painting software.
  • GIMP: Free Photoshop alternative with AI plugins.
  • Figma: UI/UX design and prototyping tool.

7. Coding & Developer Tools

  • GitHub Copilot: AI pair programmer.
  • Replit Ghostwriter: AI code generation tool.
  • Codeium: Free AI code autocomplete.
  • TabNine: AI code completion.
  • HuggingFace Models: AI/ML models library.

8. Data Science & Machine Learning Tools

  • Google Colab: Free cloud-based Python notebooks.
  • Kaggle Notebooks: ML notebooks + datasets + free GPUs.
  • HuggingFace Transformers: NLP models library.
  • Scikit-Learn: Machine learning algorithms.
  • TensorFlow & PyTorch: Deep learning frameworks.

9. Productivity & Business Tools

  • Notion AI: Write, summarize, and organize tasks.
  • Trello + Butler: Task automation.
  • Zapier: Automate apps and workflows.
  • ClickUp: AI-enhanced project management.
  • Slack AI: Workspace search & summarization.

10. Translation & Language Learning

  • DeepL: Professional-level translations.
  • Google Translate: Multi-language translation.
  • Duolingo: AI-based language learning.
  • LingvaNex: Translator + dictionary.
  • YouGlish: Real-world pronunciation examples.

11. Study & Research Tools

  • Perplexity: AI research engine with citations.
  • ChatGPT: Learning assistant for all subjects.
  • Research Rabbit: Paper discovery + visualization.
  • Semantic Scholar: Academic research search engine.
  • Zotero: Reference and citation manager.

12. Cybersecurity & Privacy Tools

  • VirusTotal: Scan files/URLs for malware.
  • Have I Been Pwned: Check for breached accounts.
  • KeePass: Offline password manager.
  • Bitwarden: Secure open-source password vault.

13. Other Useful AI Tools

  • Remove.bg: Remove backgrounds instantly.
  • CleanUp.pictures: Remove objects from photos.
  • Tome AI: AI-powered presentation creator.
  • Slidesgo AI: AI presentation templates.
  • Photopea: Online Photoshop-style editor.

While there’s no such thing as a free lunch, you can grab these free AI tools without any investment to spur your creativity, productivity and learning. No matter whether you’re working on images, videos, automation, studying or software development, the perfect AI tool will help you get your work done quicker and easier. As AI evolves, more and more powerful tools will be made available to the general public. Try out the tools above, experiment with them and find which one suits your workflow best. The future of AI is here and it’s free to everyone to use.
